# ONTO: A Protocol for Recognition. An Economy of Ideas.

## The Fracture
Innovation is gridlocked. Patent offices protect corporations, not inventors. Academic journals hold knowledge in years-long queues. Funding decisions are made in boardrooms, not by communities. Between the speed of thought and the inertia of institutions lies a fracture. 

**ONTO is a direct bridge across this fracture.**

## The ONTOnew Territory
This is not a platform. It is new ground, where rules are shaped by the natural progression of ideas.

*   **Publications.** Finished knowledge. Establish priority and share your work—without intermediaries.
*   **Ideas.** Raw thought. Stake your concept and create defensive prior art—instantly and freely.
*   **Projects.** Live development. Find resources and collaborators to bring your vision to life.

**Our axiom:** Visibility is protection. Community is strength.

## The Core: The ONTO Token
ONTO (TON network) is a currency of recognition, not a speculative asset. It is a mechanism for direct patronage.

*   **How it works:** You find work that matters to you. To support its author, you send them ONTO. Directly.
*   **What it changes:** The author gains resources to continue their work. You enact direct patronage, bypassing all bureaucratic layers.
*   **Our stance:** ONTO is a utility tool. Its primary value lies in its usefulness to the ecosystem, not in speculative promise.

## Choice and Commitment
You can support any author directly by sending any asset on the TON network to their wallet. ONTOnew.com exists for those who choose to go a step further.

In 2026 the ONTO token (TON network) will be introduced as the native currency of recognition within this ecosystem. Choosing to earn, hold, or transfer ONTO is a conscious act that does two things:

1. It directly supports an author with a liquid asset.
2. It stakes a claim on the future of this protocol. 

The value of ONTO will be a reflection of the collective belief in and growth of the ONTOnew ecosystem. By using ONTO, you are not just donating. You are voting for a future where recognition has its own economy.
